# Shipping & Delivery Policy

## Home Delivery

We offer home delivery for customers within our delivery zones. The concierge delivery experience brings your vehicle directly to your door.

### Delivery Zones & Costs

| Zone | Coverage | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Zone 1 | Within 50 miles of dealership | Free |
| Zone 2 | 51–150 miles | $299 |
| Zone 3 | 151–500 miles | $599 |
| Zone 4 | 500+ miles / Cross-country | $999 to $1,499 (quote required) |

### Delivery Timeline

- **Zone 1:** 1–3 business days after paperwork completion.
- **Zone 2:** 3–5 business days.
- **Zone 3:** 5–10 business days.
- **Zone 4:** 10–21 business days (varies by carrier).

## Delivery Process

1. **Confirm purchase** — Paperwork signed and payment processed.
2. **Pre-delivery inspection** — Vehicle inspected and detailed.
3. **Scheduling** — Delivery coordinator contacts you to confirm date and window (2-hour window provided).
4. **Delivery day** — Driver arrives with vehicle, documentation, and keys.
5. **Handoff inspection** — You and the driver walk around the vehicle together. Sign delivery receipt.

## What to Expect on Delivery Day

- Bring your driver's license and any co-signer if applicable.
- The vehicle arrives fully fueled (gasoline) or charged to 80% (EV).
- Floor mats, manuals, and all keys are included.
- A 15-minute walkthrough of key features is provided.

## Damage During Shipping

- Any damage during transport is covered by our **shipping insurance** at no cost to you.
- Document any damage during the handoff inspection before signing.
- Post-delivery damage claims must be filed within **24 hours**.

## International Shipping

We do not currently offer international shipping. For export inquiries, please contact our fleet department directly at fleet@premiumdealership.com.

## Pick-Up Option

Prefer to pick up in person? We offer complimentary valet parking and a premium handoff experience at our showroom. Notify us 48 hours in advance to ensure your vehicle is ready.
