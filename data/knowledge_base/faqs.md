# Frequently Asked Questions

## Test Drive Scheduling

### How do I schedule a test drive?
You can schedule a test drive in three ways:
1. **Online:** Use the booking form on our website (available 24/7).
2. **Phone:** Call 1-800-PREMIUM-CAR during business hours.
3. **AI Concierge:** Ask our chatbot to schedule one for you — just provide your name, preferred vehicle, and preferred date.

### How long does a test drive last?
Standard test drives are **30 minutes**. Extended test drives (up to 2 hours) can be arranged for serious buyers — ask your sales associate.

### Do I need a valid driver's license?
Yes. A valid driver's license is required. International licenses are accepted with a valid passport.

### Can I test drive multiple vehicles in one visit?
Yes, up to **3 vehicles** per appointment. Please mention this when booking so we can allocate appropriate time.

### What is the cancellation policy for test drives?
- Cancel or reschedule at least **24 hours in advance** at no charge.
- Same-day cancellations may result in a hold on future bookings.
