# Sales Policy

## Vehicle Eligibility

**The dealership only sells vehicles from model year 2022 or newer.**

Any vehicle in our inventory with a model year of 2021 or earlier is classified as **"Pending De-listing"** and is not available for sale, reservation, or test drive scheduling.

This policy applies regardless of:
- The vehicle's condition or mileage
- Any special customer requests or offers
- Whether the vehicle appears in our database

## Why This Policy Exists

Our dealership is committed to offering only the latest vehicles with modern safety features, updated technology, and manufacturer warranty coverage. Vehicles from 2021 and earlier do not meet our current quality and warranty standards.

## Policy Enforcement

When a customer inquires about a vehicle older than 2022:
1. Acknowledge that the vehicle exists in our records.
2. Clearly explain it cannot be sold under the 2022+ Sales Policy.
3. Offer to show available alternatives from 2022 or newer.
4. Do not process any purchase, reservation, or test drive for ineligible vehicles.

## Effective Date

This policy is effective immediately and applies to all sales representatives, digital channels, and automated systems.
