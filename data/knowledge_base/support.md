# Customer Support & Refund Policy

## Contact Information

- **Phone:** 1-800-PREMIUM-CAR (Mon–Fri, 9 AM – 6 PM EST)
- **Email:** support@premiumdealership.com
- **Live Chat:** Available on website during business hours
- **Emergency After-Hours:** 1-800-555-0199

## Refund Policy

### Deposit Refunds
- Reservation deposits are fully refundable within **48 hours** of placement.
- After 48 hours, deposits become non-refundable unless the vehicle is no longer available.

### Purchase Refunds
- Completed vehicle purchases may be returned within **7 days** of delivery, provided:
  - The vehicle has fewer than **200 miles** added since delivery.
  - The vehicle is returned in the same condition as delivered.
  - Original paperwork is presented.

### Refund Processing
- Refunds are processed within **5–10 business days**.
- Credit card refunds may take an additional 3–5 days depending on your bank.

## Dispute Resolution

If you are unsatisfied with a resolution:
1. Request escalation to a Senior Customer Experience Manager.
2. Disputes unresolved within 14 days may be escalated to our corporate office.
3. Written complaints can be mailed to: 123 Auto Plaza Drive, Premium City, CA 90210.

## Complaint Protocol

1. Customer contacts support via phone, email, or chat.
2. A case number is issued within 24 hours.
3. Initial response provided within 2 business days.
4. Resolution target: 7 business days.
