# Vehicle Maintenance Guide

## Electric Vehicles (EV)

### Routine Maintenance Schedule

| Service | Interval |
|---|---|
| Tire rotation | Every 7,500 miles or 6 months |
| Brake inspection | Every 15,000 miles (regenerative braking extends pad life) |
| Cabin air filter | Every 15,000 miles or 1 year |
| Battery coolant check | Every 4 years or 50,000 miles |
| Full system diagnostic | Annually |

### EV-Specific Notes
- **No oil changes required** — EVs have no combustion engine oil.
- **Brake pads last longer** due to regenerative braking reducing mechanical brake use.
- **Battery health** should be checked annually. Avoid charging to 100% daily — optimal range is 20–80%.
- **Software updates** are pushed over-the-air and should be applied promptly.

### Charging Best Practices
- Use Level 2 (AC) charging for daily home use.
- DC fast charging (Level 3) is fine for occasional use but degrades battery slightly faster.
- Avoid leaving vehicle at 0% charge for extended periods.

## Gasoline Vehicles

### Routine Maintenance Schedule

| Service | Interval |
|---|---|
| Oil change (synthetic) | Every 7,500–10,000 miles |
| Oil change (conventional) | Every 3,000–5,000 miles |
| Tire rotation | Every 5,000–7,500 miles |
| Air filter | Every 15,000–30,000 miles |
| Spark plugs | Every 30,000–60,000 miles |
| Transmission fluid | Every 30,000–60,000 miles |
| Brake fluid | Every 2 years |
| Coolant flush | Every 30,000 miles or 2 years |

### Gasoline-Specific Notes
- Always use the manufacturer-recommended fuel grade (check owner's manual).
- Premium vehicles typically require 91+ octane fuel.
- Low tire pressure significantly impacts fuel economy.

---

## Hybrid Vehicles

Hybrid vehicles follow **gasoline maintenance schedules** for the combustion engine components, but benefit from reduced brake wear similar to EVs. Battery checks are recommended every 3 years.

---

## Warranty Coverage

All new 2022+ vehicles come with:
- **3-year / 36,000-mile** bumper-to-bumper warranty
- **5-year / 60,000-mile** powertrain warranty
- EVs additionally: **8-year / 100,000-mile** battery warranty
